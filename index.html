About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in AI for Science, with a core focus on the application of Large Language Models (LLMs) to scientific challenges. I also have a strong research interest in the emerging capabilities of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) agents.
