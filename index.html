Yuxiang Xu

Welcome to my webpage! My name is Yuxiang Xu, and I am currently a third-year graduate student at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. I am also a member of the CAS Key Laboratory of Microgravity.

My research is dedicated to leveraging deep learning to support significant scientific projects, with a current focus on space-based gravitational wave detection missions such as LISA and Taiji. I am particularly interested in the applications of generative AI in these space-based gravitational wave detection tasks and am keen on integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) into this field. If you share this interest, I welcome you to contact me and engage in discussions!

I have also spent some time on high-performance data simulation based on CUDA.
